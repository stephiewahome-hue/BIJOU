Step-by-Step Guide

1

Add items to your cart & place order

Browse the shop, add your favourite products to cart, then tap Checkout via WhatsApp. Your order list is sent to us automatically.

2

We confirm availability & your total

We'll reply on WhatsApp within minutes to confirm your items are in stock and give you the exact amount to pay.

3

Send 75% deposit via M-Pesa

Go to M-Pesa → Send Money and send the deposit to:

0740 772 900

Use your name as the reference so we can match your payment.

4

Send us your M-Pesa confirmation SMS

Forward the M-Pesa confirmation message to us on WhatsApp so we can verify and start processing your order.

5

We dispatch your order 🎉

Once payment is confirmed we pack and dispatch your order. We'll send tracking updates on WhatsApp. Pay the remaining 25% on delivery.
